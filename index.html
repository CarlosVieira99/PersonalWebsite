Areas of Expertise & Technical Skills

Digital Electronics

Having designed and produced 4 different PCB projects, this is my most comfortable area of knowledge. I have some experience programming several microcontrollers in C like the ATmega328P, the STM32F103 and the ESP32. Consequently I have also developed a reasonable amount of libraries for sensors and drivers to be used with this microcontrollers.

Robotics & Control

This is my current field of study. I have been developing simulations and real world operations using ROS (Robot Operation System). Several fields of study are included in here, like Image Processing, System Control, Project Management, and others. I'm currently working along the UNEXUP project team, an underwater robot for flooded mines.

Web Development

Mostly self-taught (or online taught) at this field, where I have gained some interest due to the limitless possibilities of creation and innovation using websites and general networks. A proof of this self interest is the development of this website, where I had to develop skills around HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and SQL. IoT applications are also appealing.

Apps & UI

In love with Python since I started programming it, it is so universal and flexible that you can do practically anything with it. This motivated me to learn more about UI (User Interface) with the help of QT Designer and also the development of 3D real-time simulations using VTK (Visual ToolKit) and mathematical analysis with MatPlotLib and Numpy.

Industrial Automation

A recent interest in my life after having the opportunity to automate some systems at ISEP. I had the chance to program Siemens and Schneider PLC's, using respectively, the software SIMATIC Step 7 and SoMachine. For this I used the LADDER programming language. One day I hope to build a PLC of my own for academic purposes.

CAD Design

After acquiring a 3D printer, I challenged myself to create and implement physical structures to help creating solid and robust products. During this process I developed some skills in CAD drawing and general knowledge about 3D printers and addictive manufacturing. In the beginning of last year I had printed around 500 face shields for health care professionals.
